Topics

  • Big Data, AI / ML

    Collecting Big Data, analysing and using it to understand and predict the customer behaviour.

  • E-commerce

    How to keep the operations running, cut off the burdens, keep the team and sales working.

  • Cloud Tech

    how the companies can use the cloud technologies to run their business online.

  • Fintech

    New solutions for business, retail banking, digital banking, startups & banks.

  • Travel & Hospitality

    How to prepare for the boom after the crisis, will the boom actually be there, next season expectations.

  • Remote work

    How the crisis will make us more global and open new opportunities; going global - new wave.

  • Cybersecurity

    Protecting the company data while working online, employee privacy issues.

  • Startups survival

    What the investors think, what the founders should know to survive.
